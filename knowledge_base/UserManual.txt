TECHMART ELECTRONICS - USER MANUAL
====================================

WELCOME TO TECHMART ELECTRONICS
This manual covers account setup, the TechMart dashboard, subscription management,
and how to get the most out of your TechMart products and services.

---

CHAPTER 1 - CREATING AND SETTING UP YOUR ACCOUNT

1. Visit techmart.example and click Sign Up.
2. Enter your full name, email address, and a strong password.
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - Must include at least one number and one special character
3. Check your email for a verification link and click it within 24 hours.
4. Complete your profile by adding your phone number and billing address.
5. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) under Settings > Security for added protection.

Password requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (e.g., !, @, #, $)

If you forget your password, click Forgot Password on the login page and follow the instructions.
A reset link will be sent to your email and expires after 30 minutes.

CHAPTER 3 - MANAGING YOUR SUBSCRIPTION

To upgrade or downgrade your plan:
1. Go to My Products > Manage Subscription.
2. Select the new plan and click Switch Plan.
3. Upgrades take effect immediately. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder
   of the current billing cycle.
4. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to My Products > Manage Subscription > Cancel Plan.
2. Select a cancellation reason and confirm.
3. Your subscription remains active until the end of the paid period.

Auto-renewal:
Subscriptions auto-renew by default. To disable auto-renewal, go to Billing > Auto-Renewal
and toggle it off. You will receive a reminder email 7 days before your renewal date.

CHAPTER 5 - CLOUD STORAGE AND FILE MANAGEMENT

Storage limits:
- Basic: 10 GB
- Pro: 100 GB
- Premium: 1 TB

Files are automatically synced when connected to the internet.
Offline access can be enabled by right-clicking a file and selecting Make Available Offline.
File deletions are recoverable from the Trash folder for 30 days.

Sharing files:
Click the Share button on any file. Enter the recipient's email address and set permissions:
- View only
- Comment
- Edit

Shared links can be set to expire after 1, 7, or 30 days.

CHAPTER 6 - SECURITY BEST PRACTICES

- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) using an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator.
- Never share your password or licence key with anyone, including TechMart staff.
- Use a unique password for your TechMart account.
- Log out of shared or public devices after use via Settings > Security > Sign Out All Devices.
- Review active sessions under Settings > Security > Active Sessions and revoke unfamiliar ones.
- Do not click suspicious links claiming to be from TechMart. We will never ask for your
  password via email or chat.

CHAPTER 9 - HARDWARE CARE TIPS (PROBOOK SERIES)

- Clean the screen with a soft, dry microfibre cloth. Do not use liquids directly on the screen.
- Keep vents unobstructed to prevent overheating. Do not use on soft surfaces like beds or sofas.
- Charge using only the official TechMart 65W USB-C charger or a certified Thunderbolt 4 charger.
- Store the laptop in a temperature range of 0°C to 35°C (32°F to 95°F).
- Do not expose to extreme humidity or direct sunlight for extended periods.
- For long-term storage (more than 3 months), charge the battery to 50% before storing.
- Battery calibration: let the battery drain fully once per month for best longevity.
