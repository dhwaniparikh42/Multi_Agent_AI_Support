TECHMART ELECTRONICS - REFUND AND RETURN POLICY
================================================

OVERVIEW
TechMart Electronics offers a 30-day return window on all eligible products.
Our goal is to ensure every customer is fully satisfied with their purchase.
This policy applies to all orders placed on techmart.example.

---

ELIGIBILITY FOR RETURNS
- Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
- Products must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging.
- All accessories, cables, manuals, and warranty cards must be included.
- A valid proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.

---

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
- Software licences and digital downloads once activated.
- Gift cards.
- Items marked Final Sale at the time of purchase.
- Products with physical damage caused by the customer.
- Items missing serial numbers or with tampered stickers.

---

HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
Step 1: Log into your TechMart account and go to My Orders.
Step 2: Select the order and click Request Return.
Step 3: Choose the items and provide a reason for the return.
Step 4: Print the prepaid return shipping label sent to your email.
Step 5: Pack the item securely and drop it at any authorised courier location.

---

REFUND TIMELINE
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will send you a confirmation email.
Refunds are processed within 2 business days of inspection approval.

- Credit/Debit card: 5-7 business days to appear on your statement.
- PayPal: 3-5 business days.
- TechMart Store Credit: Instant.
- Bank transfer: 7-10 business days.

---

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us within 7 days of delivery at
support@techmart.example with photos of the damage. We will arrange a free replacement or
full refund, including return shipping costs. You do not need to return the damaged item.

---

EXCHANGES
We do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange a product, return the original item for a refund
and place a new order for the replacement. This ensures the fastest processing time.

---

SUBSCRIPTION REFUNDS
Software subscription plans may be cancelled at any time.
- Cancelled within 7 days of billing date: full refund issued.
- Cancelled after 7 days: takes effect at end of current billing cycle, no partial refund.
- Annual plans cancelled within 30 days: prorated refund for unused months.

---

RETURN SHIPPING
- Defective or incorrectly shipped items: TechMart covers return shipping at no cost.
- Change of mind returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping cost ($6.99 standard label).
- International returns: Customer is responsible for all return shipping and customs fees.
