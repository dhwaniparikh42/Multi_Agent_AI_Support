ORDER PROCESSING
Orders placed before 2:00 pm EST on a business day are processed the same day.
Orders placed after 2:00 pm EST, or on weekends and public holidays, are processed the next business day.
You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order is dispatched.
During peak seasons (November-December), processing may take an additional 1-2 business days.

---

TRACKING YOUR ORDER
All orders include free tracking. Your tracking number will be emailed within 24 hours of shipment.
You can also view tracking in your TechMart account under My Orders.
Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear after shipment.

---

LOST OR STOLEN PACKAGES
If your tracking shows Delivered but you have not received your package:
1. Check with neighbours and building management.
2. Wait 24 hours in case of early delivery scans.
3. Contact us at support@techmart.example with your order number.
We will open a carrier investigation within 1 business day.
If the package is confirmed lost, we will reship or issue a full refund at no extra cost.

---

ADDRESS CHANGES
Shipping addresses can only be changed before the order is shipped.
Contact support@techmart.example immediately after placing the order to request a change.
Once shipped, address changes are at the carrier's discretion and may incur additional fees.

---

PO BOXES AND APO/FPO ADDRESSES
Standard shipping is available to PO boxes and APO/FPO addresses via USPS.
Express and overnight shipping are not available for these addresses.
Delivery to APO/FPO addresses may take 7-21 days depending on location.
