TECHMART ELECTRONICS - INSTALLATION GUIDE
==========================================

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
Requirement          | Minimum                      | Recommended
Operating System     | Windows 10 64-bit            | Windows 11
Processor            | Intel Core i3 / AMD Ryzen 3  | Intel Core i5 / Ryzen 5
RAM                  | 4 GB                         | 8 GB or more
Storage              | 2 GB free space              | 5 GB free space
Internet             | Required for activation      | Broadband 10 Mbps+
Browser (Web App)    | Chrome 100+, Firefox 100+    | Latest version of any major browser

---

STEP 1 - DOWNLOAD THE INSTALLER
Log into your TechMart account at techmart.example.
Go to My Products and click Download next to your software.
The installer file will download to your Downloads folder:
- Windows: TechMartSetup.exe
- Mac: TechMartSetup.dmg
- Linux: TechMartSetup.deb or TechMartSetup.rpm

Verify the download by checking the file size matches what is shown on the download page.

---

STEP 2 - RUN THE INSTALLER

Windows:
Double-click TechMartSetup.exe.
If prompted by User Account Control (UAC), click Yes to allow the installation.
Do not cancel at the UAC prompt or the installation will fail.

Mac:
Open TechMartSetup.dmg.
Drag the TechMart icon to your Applications folder.
Open TechMart from the Applications folder.
If macOS blocks the app, go to System Preferences > Security and Privacy and click Open Anyway.

---

STEP 3 - ACCEPT THE LICENCE AGREEMENT
Read the End User Licence Agreement (EULA).
Click I Agree to proceed.
If you do not agree, click Cancel to exit the installer.

---

STEP 4 - CHOOSE INSTALLATION FOLDER
The default installation path is:
- Windows: C:\Program Files\TechMart
- Mac: /Applications/TechMart

You may click Browse to choose a different location.
We recommend using the default path to avoid permission issues.
Click Install to begin. Installation typically takes 2-5 minutes.

---

STEP 5 - ACTIVATE YOUR LICENCE
Once installed, launch TechMart and sign in with your account credentials.
Your subscription will activate automatically upon sign-in.
If activation fails:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Ensure your subscription is active at techmart.example/account.
3. Check that your system clock shows the correct date and time.
You may also enter your licence key manually under Settings > Account > Enter Licence Key.

---

STEP 6 - INSTALL UPDATES
After installation, the application will check for updates automatically.
Install all available updates before use to ensure best performance and security.
Updates can also be triggered manually via Help > Check for Updates.
Auto-updates can be enabled under Settings > General > Automatically install updates.

---

TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON INSTALLATION ERRORS

Error: Installation failed - insufficient permissions
Solution: Right-click the installer and select Run as Administrator.

Error: VCRUNTIME140.dll is missing
Solution: Download and install the Microsoft Visual C++ Redistributable 2015-2022
from the official Microsoft website.

Error: This app cannot run on your PC
Solution: Ensure you downloaded the correct version. Check if your system is 32-bit or 64-bit
at Settings > System > About. Download the matching version from techmart.example.

Antivirus blocking installation
Solution: Temporarily disable real-time protection during installation, then re-enable it.
Add TechMart to your antivirus whitelist after installation.

Licence activation fails after installation
Solution: Ensure your subscription is active and your internet connection is working.
Try logging out and back in. If the issue persists, contact support@techmart.example.

Installation hangs or freezes
Solution: Wait 10 minutes before force-closing. If still frozen, restart your computer and
run the installer again. Disable any VPN during installation.

---

SILENT INSTALLATION (FOR IT ADMINISTRATORS)
Run the installer from command line:
TechMartSetup.exe /silent /norestart

This installs with default settings and no user interaction. Useful for mass deployment.
For Group Policy deployment, contact sales@techmart.example for the MSI package.

---

UNINSTALLING TECHMART SOFTWARE
Windows: Go to Settings > Apps > Installed Apps, find TechMart, and click Uninstall.
Mac: Open Finder > Applications, right-click TechMart, and select Move to Trash.
Your account data and cloud-saved files are not deleted when you uninstall.
