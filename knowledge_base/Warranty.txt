TECHMART ELECTRONICS - WARRANTY INFORMATION
===========================================

STANDARD LIMITED WARRANTY
All TechMart Electronics hardware products are covered by a 1-year Limited Warranty from the
date of original purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under
normal use conditions.

---

WHAT IS COVERED
- Manufacturing defects in hardware components.
- Faulty display screens (dead pixels exceeding 5 per panel).
- Battery defects (capacity falling below 80% within the first year under normal use).
- Keyboard, trackpad, and port malfunctions not caused by physical damage.
- Internal component failures (RAM, SSD, motherboard) under normal use.
- Software bugs present at the time of purchase.

---

WHAT IS NOT COVERED
- Physical damage from drops, spills, or misuse.
- Damage from unauthorised modifications or repairs.
- Cosmetic damage such as scratches, dents, or broken plastic.
- Damage caused by operating outside permitted environmental conditions.
- Consumable parts such as batteries after 1 year of use.
- Software issues, viruses, or data loss caused by user actions.
- Theft or loss of the device.
- Damage caused by third-party accessories not approved by TechMart.

---

EXTENDED WARRANTY - TECHMART CARE+
TechMart Care+ extends your warranty coverage and adds accidental damage protection.

Plan                                          | Price (one-time)
TechMart Care+ 2-Year                        | $79.99
TechMart Care+ 3-Year                        | $129.99
TechMart Care+ 3-Year with Accidental Damage | $179.99

TechMart Care+ with Accidental Damage covers:
- Screen cracks and broken displays from drops.
- Liquid damage from spills.
- One accidental damage claim per year.
- All standard warranty coverage.

TechMart Care+ must be purchased within 30 days of the product purchase date.

---

HOW TO CLAIM YOUR WARRANTY
Step 1: Contact support@techmart.example or call 1-800-555-0100.
Step 2: Provide your order number, serial number, and a description of the issue.
Step 3: Our team will diagnose the issue remotely (usually within 24 hours).
Step 4: If the issue cannot be resolved remotely, we will issue a prepaid shipping label.
Step 5: Send the device to our repair centre. Repair or replacement takes 5-10 business days.
Step 6: Your device will be returned to you with free shipping.

For accidental damage claims under TechMart Care+, photos of the damage are required before
sending the device in for repair.

---

WARRANTY TRANSFERABILITY
The TechMart standard 1-year warranty is transferable to a new owner if the product is sold
or gifted within the warranty period. The warranty period does not reset upon transfer.
TechMart Care+ extended warranty plans are non-transferable.

---

OUT-OF-WARRANTY REPAIRS
For devices outside the warranty period, TechMart offers paid repair services.
- Diagnostic fee: $49 (waived if repair is approved)
- Screen replacement: $149 - $249 depending on model
- Battery replacement: $79
- Keyboard replacement: $99
- Motherboard repair: $199 - $349

Contact warranty@techmart.example for an out-of-warranty repair quote.
